Oregon State University-Cascades

General Information

Oregon State University-Cascades is located in Bend, Oregon. It's been handing diplomas out since 2001 and is a very affordable college for those in the area. Because of its location, attendees are often participants in snow sports, such as skiing and snowboarding. It offers a computer science major, a software engineering option, and a web and mobile software development option.

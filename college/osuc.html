Oregon State University-Cascades

General Information

Oregon State University-Cascades is located in Bend, Oregon. It's been handing diplomas out since 2001 and is a very affordable college for those in the area. Because of its location, attendees are often participants in snow sports, such as skiing and snowboarding. It offers a computer science major, a software engineering option, and a web and mobile software development option.
Learn more »


Career Options

Computer science career options at OSU-Cascades typically guide students towards careers in software engineering and also prepares students interested in starting their own software businesses or join software startups. The available options include Software Engineering and Software Entrepreneurship.


Graduate programs

OSU-Cascades offers a graduate program for software engineering and Software Entrepreneurship and focuses on growing your ability to analyze problems, function effectivily in teams, and several other critical skills in a work place. PGS page.


What makes OSU any better than anywhere else?

Students attending will have the oppurtinity to meet and build connections with students and faculty which can help land them a job in the area along with the chance to start up a successful start up in the area. The program will also provide you with a solid foundation in computer science, as well as industry relevent skills and technical experience, as explained under the Career Options heading. They also offer an option specifically for COCC students and credit transfering as long as some requirements in relation to GPA.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Requirements

You will need 180 quarter credits to graduate, including 60 upper-division credits. This includes the Baccalaurate core: 32-38 credits, Computer Science: 97 credits, Applied Computer Science Option: 32 credits, and any other elective credits to meet the 180 credits.
